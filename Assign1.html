Scrabbled eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite food. Here is the recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1tbs Butter
  • 2tbs Cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in frying pan over medium flame.
  2. Gently mix the egg and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter is melted add cream and eggs.
  4. Using spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center for 20 seconds.
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from heat.
